# Conway's Game of Life

Quant CLUB

</div>

The functionalities are as follows:

* To zoom in, press the '+' key or the '=' key on the keyboard.
* To zoom out, press the '-' key on the keyboard.
* To reset the grid, press the 'c' key on the keyboard.
* To randomize the grid based on the provided seed and probability, press the 's' key on the keyboard.
* To start the simulation in a continuous manner, press the spacebar on the keyboard.
* To pause the simulation, press the spacebar again.
* To manually advance one generation, press the 'Right Arrow' key.
* **Price Plotting**: Press the 'p' key to toggle a separate window showing the "Price" (vertical Center of Mass) plotted over time.
